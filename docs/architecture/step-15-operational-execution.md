# Step 15 — Operational Execution

Step 15 converts approved GTM programs into accountable daily work. Work items may originate from campaign steps, research, signals, opportunity stage requirements, or approvals. Opportunities retain source, value, probability, owner and next action. Approval requests are immutable governance records with snapshots and reviewer-role requirements.

Production commands must be transactional and auditable. UI controls must not directly mutate multiple execution objects independently.
