# Step 42 — Post-GA Operations, Adoption Analytics, and Continuous Improvement

Step 42 establishes the operating system after General Availability. It measures adoption against eligible populations, tracks customer health and value realization, governs feedback intake, and converts verified production evidence into prioritized release initiatives.

## Control model

- Adoption metrics include owners, eligible populations, targets, prior-period comparisons, and trends.
- Customer health combines adoption, support risk, realized value, accountable ownership, and next actions.
- Feedback cannot disappear into an ungoverned backlog; every item has source, impact, votes, status, and owner.
- Value outcomes compare a documented baseline with current and target performance and require verification.
- Improvement initiatives are ranked by impact-to-effort ratio and linked to customer evidence and a target release.
- The next release is considered planning-ready only when the composite score is at least 80, critical feedback is controlled, and an approved or active v1.1 initiative exists.
